# AlgoDeck AI Image Generation Prompts

These prompts are designed for ChatGPT (DALL-E 3) to create professional, high-performance pre-launch marketing visuals.

## Branding Guidelines to include in all prompts:
- **Color Palette:** Deep navy background (#0A0E27), vibrant electric blue (#3A7BFF), neon purple, and cyan (#17B7BD).
- **Style:** High-tech, sleek, futuristic, professional, clean UI.
- **Theme:** Mobile-first trading, automation, precision, security.

---

## 1. App Lifestyle & Showcase
*Use these to show the mobile experience.*

- **Prompt:** A professional individual holding a modern smartphone in a dimly lit, high-end contemporary office. The smartphone screen clearly displays a clean, sleek mobile app interface with trading charts and bot statistics. The lighting is cinematic, featuring soft electric blue and purple ambient glow reflected on the screen and workspace. The background is a sophisticated, blurry dark navy environment. Photorealistic, 8k, professional photography style.

## 2. Abstract "Custom Bot" Technology
*Use these to visualize the power of custom automation.*

- **Prompt:** A 3D abstract representation of a custom algorithmic trading bot. Sleek, minimalist chrome structure with flowing neon blue and vibrant purple light trails weaving through it, symbolizing data flow and automated logic. High-tech, futuristic aesthetic, isolated on a dark navy (#0A0E27) background. Focus on precision and high performance. 3D render, octane render, detailed, sharp edges.

## 3. Pre-Launch Teaser / Hype
*Use these to build excitement.*

- **Prompt:** A dramatic close-up of a mobile phone screen resting on a dark, reflective surface. The screen shows a "Coming Soon" or "AlgoDeck Waitlist" landing page with a countdown or a glowing "Join" button. The room is dark, illuminated only by a vibrant, pulsating purple and blue light emanating from the phone's screen. The aesthetic is mysterious, sleek, and high-performance. 8k, photorealistic, depth of field.

---

## Tips for Best Results:
- **Aspect Ratio:** If you need these for social media, append `--ar 16:9` (for landscape) or `--ar 9:16` (for mobile/stories) if using tools like Midjourney, or simply ask ChatGPT for "a vertical image for a mobile screen" or "a wide image for a website banner."
- **Consistency:** If you find a style you like, use "In the same style as the previous image" for your subsequent prompts.
- **Refinement:** If an image is too busy, add: "Minimalist composition, clean UI, focus on the phone screen."
