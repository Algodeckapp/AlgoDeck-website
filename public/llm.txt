# AlgoDeck - Mobile-First Trading Automation Platform

## Business Overview
AlgoDeck is a modern, high-performance mobile trading automation platform designed for individual traders. We empower users to build, backtest, and deploy their own custom trading bots directly to their MetaTrader 4 (MT4) and MetaTrader 5 (MT5) accounts.

## Core Value Proposition (What Sets Us Apart)
- Mobile-First Approach: Unlike traditional trading automation solutions that are desktop-heavy, AlgoDeck is built from the ground up for mobile (Android & iOS). You control your entire trading empire from your pocket.
- Custom Bot Builder: We give you the tools to turn your unique trading ideas into functional, automated bots without writing a single line of code.
- Seamless MT4/MT5 Integration: Direct, secure connectivity to MT4/MT5 accounts allows for instant deployment of your custom strategies.
- Risk Management: Built-in tools for automated risk management (stop-loss, take-profit, max drawdown) to protect your capital.

## Target Audience
Individual traders, retail investors, and automated trading enthusiasts who want professional-grade tools to execute their own custom strategies with the convenience of a mobile app.

## Key Features
- Visual Strategy Builder: An intuitive interface for defining your own specific rules, logic, and conditions for your custom bots.
- Cloud-Based Bot Hosting: Your custom strategies run in the cloud, ensuring your bots are active 24/7 without needing to keep a desktop application or VPS running.
- Real-Time Analytics: Monitor backtest results, live performance, and risk metrics on the go.
- Secure API Authentication: Robust security for broker account connectivity.
